About

Bayview, Inc. is a California Mutual-Benefit Non-Profit Corporation, operating as a California Compassionate Use Collective, in compliance with CA Prop. 215, SB 420, and Sections 11362.4 and 11362.7 of California Health and Safety Code.

We are a collective of patients, growers and practitioners, who view cannabis as a powerfully effective medicine.
